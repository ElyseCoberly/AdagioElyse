Making Kids Musical!

Music is a crucial part of a child's education. Learning music improves language and math ability, as well as fine and gross motor skills. And of course, provides a child the opportunity to develop a lifelong passion for the arts.

In our classes, children learn at a pace and in a method that is pedagogically proven and suited to their individual development. We explore sounds and pattern through imitation, movement, and instruments. For younger children, a caregiver will stay throughout the class, while for older children, caregivers are welcome to visit together--we have quite a Scrabble rivalry developing!
